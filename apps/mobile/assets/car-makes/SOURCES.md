# Car make logo sources

The images in this directory are normalized, transparent PNG renditions used
only to identify vehicle makes in the mobile picker.

- Most source images: `filippofilip95/car-logos-dataset` (MIT-licensed dataset
  tooling and curation; individual logos remain trademarks of their owners).
- BAIC and DFSK source vectors: `car-brand-logos@1.0.0`.
- GAC, Kaiyi, Kia, and KGM source files: Wikimedia Commons.

All brand names and logos remain the property of their respective trademark
owners.
